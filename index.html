FAQ

Common questions

How does TutorCat match students?

TutorCat compares the student's level, goals, subject or counseling focus, destination, preferred format, urgency, and profile fit.

Can parents join the session?

Yes. Parent participation is especially useful for education counseling, study destination planning, and younger students.

Do you offer country-specific counseling?

Yes. TutorCat can route families to counselors or mentors with experience connected to destinations such as the US, Canada, Germany, Netherlands, Czech Republic, UK, Australia, and UAE.

Can I choose a counselor who studied in the country?

You can request that preference. Profile details show relevant study, work, or advising experience when available.

Is this admissions or immigration advice?

No. Counseling sessions are educational guidance only and do not replace official admissions, visa, legal, or immigration advice.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes. Many profiles support online sessions, and some support in-person or hybrid formats depending on location.

How do I request a tutor introduction?

Use the matching wizard, a profile request button, or the contact form. Each opens a prefilled email you can review before sending.
